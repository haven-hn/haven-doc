## The Path to Privacy (Our Roadmap)
We are evolving through three distinct phases to ensure a balance between immediate utility, compliance, and long-term anonymity:

* **Phase 1 (Currently Live):** Prepaid virtual Mastercards for instant use.
* **Phase 2 (Under Development):** Direct spending from self-custodial wallets.
* **Phase 3 (Est. ~Q4 2026):** Full-stack private financial management.

## The Haven Card

### Overview
The Haven Card is a virtual Mastercard designed for instant issuance and global spending. It allows you to convert your on-chain assets into a spendable balance.

### Key Features
* **Format:** Virtual Credit Card only (for instant deployment to digital wallets). Other options are under development.
* **No KYC:** We do not require IDs, Proof of Address (KYC), or verify user data. Users simply provide basic self-declared information required to activate Apple Pay, Google Pay, or Samsung Pay. This data is strictly used for wallet provisioning and remains completely decoupled from your transaction history and account profile.
* **Seamless Onboarding:** Your card becomes available shortly after your funds are successfully loaded.
* **Network:** Mastercard.

### Supported Networks
You can fund your Haven card with stablecoins from the following networks:
* **Fuel** (Optimized for zero-fee issuance)
* **Ethereum Mainnet**
* **Arbitrum**
* **Base**

## Usage & Restrictions

### Restricted Regions
The Haven Card is accepted globally, excluding the following regions:
* Cuba, North Korea, Iran, Syria, Crimea, Afghanistan, Belarus, Russian Federation, Sudan, and Venezuela.

### Restricted Merchants
Haven works at millions of merchants worldwide, but is currently restricted at the following:
* **Cash & Crypto:** Money orders, wire transfers, cryptocurrency purchases, and loan repayments.
* **Fuel:** Gas stations and automated fuel dispensers.
* **iGaming & Adult:** Gambling, betting, and adult entertainment.
* **Gaming:** Video game arcades and digital game purchases.
* **Regulated Goods:** Pharmacies, drug wholesalers, and tobacco shops.

## Referrals Program

### How it Works
Share your unique invite codes to earn rewards when friends on your network join the ecosystem and purchase a card.

* **The Reward:** Earn $5 USDC credited toward your next card purchase.
* **The Trigger:** Rewards are issued once your referred friend activates a card with a minimum of $50 USD.
